Maria-Teresa
De Rosa Palmini

PhD Researcher · University of Zurich

I am a 3rd-year PhD researcher at the Digital Society Initiative at the University of Zurich, working within the project The Canon of Latent Spaces: How Large AI Models Encode Art and Culture under the guidance of Dr. Eva Cetinić.

My research focuses on evaluation benchmarking and explainability for multimodal AI models — investigating how generative systems learn, represent, and reproduce cultural and societal knowledge, and how users interpret AI-generated content. I draw on methods from machine learning, digital humanities, and human–AI interaction to support AI that is more transparent, interpretable, and aligned with human values.

Interests  Generative AI · Multimodal Models · Fairness & Bias · Societal Impact of AI · Model Evaluation & Explainability
